About StrainRx

For Patients

StrainRx is a resource for medical marijuana patients. We help users identify and locate cannabis strains ideally suited for specific purposes, be it to treat a condition or bring about some desirable effect that cannabis is known to produce.

Our recommendation engine asks 4 questions to determine the user's ideal preferences. Based on the answers, a list of strains matching those preferences is generated. Think of this engine as a really smart bud-tender, capable of answering all of your questions at any time!

Once our user knows what strains are optimal, it is time to find them! StrainRx enables the filtering of our recommendations based on a user's location, so that they could find their favorite buds whenever, and wherever they may be.

To ensure that our menus are always accurate and up-to-date, we give users the power to modify vendor inventory, so that any patient who encounters an 'out of stock' issue can update the system in addition to providing ratings and reviews.

We also introduce a dynamic feedback feature around strain details and can identify and incorporate significant changes automatically, ensuring that the knowledge base is always learning, improving, and reflecting patient preferences over time.

With StrainRx, new as well as experienced patients can discover new strains, read and write reviews, compare prices, or find new vendors. The site also hosts one of the largest and most frequently updated cannabis databases on the internet and as such it is one of the best places to look up a strain or find something new to try.

For Vendors

StrainRx enables cannabis vendors to showcase their inventory to patients who are looking to find it.

We make it easy to sign up, create and maintain your inventory, manage multiple locations and feature special deals, offers or promotions. For delivery services, we provide easy controls over how far you'll go. Whether you have a large operation or just a small shop with quality buds, we'll make it possible to find the right customers immediately.
